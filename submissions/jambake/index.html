A Jazz Drummers' Top 5

I grew up playing drums, and not in the most typical way a young drummer is thought as.

Jazz was the genre of choice for me, and to this day my favorite music and style of playing is jazz.

Here is a list of my top five jazz songs:

  1. "All Blues" - Miles Davis
  2. "Take Five" - Dave Bruebeck
  3. "Autumn Leaves" - Cannonball Adderley
  4. "Moanin'" - Art Blakey
  5. "Blue Train" - John Coltrane
