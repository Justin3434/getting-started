Life, A Sum

As I sit here and consider where I want this first assignment to go, I've come to a very reflective place. The past month has been one of transitions, so it seems only appropriate that I consider how my path is currently being shaped by my last two years of experience as an AmeriCorps service member in St. Louis.

A Complete Overhaul

Can only be...

  • Well-informed
  • Confusing
  • Hapfully considered
  • Heartbreaking
  • Everything you (and I) need.

My time in STL as an AmeriCorps volunteer was undoubtedly representative of this list. So, now that the term is over, I find need to put down a few thoughts about my service. I found so much fulfillment in new experience but also grieved my old self, put away. These two years truly brought me to a space where I felt the drive to force change where federal, state, institutional, and organizational red tape inhibits and denounces self-advocacy. It's hard for me to move on from everything I've helped build. But, there's always next, which I'm calling:

& What? (WIth some music that inspires me.)

  • Advocacy.
  • Create.
  • Intentionality.
