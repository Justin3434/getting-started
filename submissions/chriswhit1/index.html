My International Travels(France & Japan)

Twenty years from now you will be more disappointed by the things you did not do than by the ones you did do.-Mark Twain

I got accepted to an agriculture program in France

I was the only one that signed up to do the program in my college. I had to take the plane by myself. I was very nervous. When I got off the plane in a new country I took a deep breath and was able to find my way

I went to many tourist sites and visited many farm. I made a lot of friends and will always cheerish that trip.

I went with a student and a couple professors to Hiroshima, Japan

Since I was the only one in my agriculture department that went to France, I was given an opportunity to go to Japan and mingle with some agriculture students

Japan was beautiful and I loved the food. Everyone was very nice and we built a good relationship with a super science high school.

The goal of the trip was to establish a relationship to promote exchanging students.
